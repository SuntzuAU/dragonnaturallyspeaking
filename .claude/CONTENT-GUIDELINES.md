# Content Guidelines - Legal Compliance and Voice

Applies to all content across the VRA gateway site network.

## Australian Consumer Law (ACL)
- No misleading or deceptive statements
- Mark unverified claims with `[VERIFY]`
- Pricing requires two independent sources + verification date + disclaimer
- No absolute superiority claims

## Healthcare Content
Must NOT imply software improves patient outcomes, provides clinical advice, is a medical device, or is clinically validated.

## Evidence
Never invent statistics, research, quotes, testimonials, or case studies. Cite sources or mark `[VERIFY SOURCE]`.

## Authority Language
All VRA/Russell claims must come from `src/data/link-network.json` -> `authority` block. Never improvise.

## Voice
Authoritative, friendly, direct, honest. Australian spelling. Avoid: best, ultimate, guaranteed, revolutionary, proven. Prefer: may help, can assist, commonly used for, designed to support, practical, reliable.

## Approval Workflow
Draft in chat. Flag [VERIFY]. Owner approves. Owner says "commit it". Never commit without approval. Never generate images without approval.
